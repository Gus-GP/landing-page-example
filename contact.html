Common Questions

What happens in a discovery session?

A discovery session is a brief, no-pressure conversation where we discuss your current goals and challenges, and I answer any questions you have about coaching. It's an opportunity for us both to determine if we're a good fit.

How long are coaching engagements?

Coaching journeys typically run 3-4 months with 9 sessions. Single clarity sessions are also available, and organizational engagements are customized based on needs.

Are sessions virtual or in-person?

All coaching sessions are conducted virtually via video call, allowing for flexibility and convenience regardless of location.

Do you work with organizations?

Yes! I partner with organizations for executive coaching, leadership development, team facilitation, and change leadership support. Contact me to discuss your organization's needs.
