A Thoughtful Partner in Growth

I'm a leadership and personal development coach who works with ambitious professionals stepping into new levels of responsibility and growth. I help people move forward with clarity while staying aligned with what matters most. I'm especially drawn to those who want their success to feel intentional, not accidental.

As a first-generation professional and working mom, I know what it takes to pursue meaningful goals without losing sight of your values, relationships, and wellbeing.

Over the past decade, I've led organizational development and leadership strategy work at Lockheed Martin, supporting leaders through growth, change, and complex transitions. That experience informs a coaching style grounded in strategic clarity and thoughtful reflection.

I believe growth should not require sacrificing what matters most. Ambition and alignment are not opposing forces — they can and should coexist. The most effective leadership begins with self-awareness and intentional action.

My Approach

My coaching blends practical strategy with mindset and perspective work to help clients expand how they see themselves and their possibilities. Real growth rarely comes from quick fixes. Instead, I create space for thoughtful reflection, honest exploration, and forward movement rooted in clarity and intention.
